Policies

Terms & Conditions

Reservations & Deposits

A non-refundable $150 deposit is required to secure your rental date. Your date is not confirmed until the deposit is received. The remaining balance of $100 is due upon delivery. Failure to pay the balance at delivery will result in the unit not being set up.

Cancellations

Deposits are non-refundable. If you cancel 6 or more days before your event, your deposit may be applied toward a future rental date within one calendar year. Cancellations within 5 days of the event forfeit the deposit entirely.

Weather Policy

Weather cancellations must be made by 7am the morning of your scheduled delivery. We do not deliver if wind speeds are 15 mph or greater, or if there is a 30% or greater chance of rain. If the unit has already been delivered, the customer is responsible for full payment regardless of weather. Temperatures above 100°F may result in cancellation at our discretion.

Setup Requirements

We do not set up on rock, gravel, or uneven surfaces. A standard electrical outlet must be within 50 feet of the setup location on a dedicated circuit. If you are at a park without electricity, you must provide a generator. Customer is responsible for clearing the setup area of debris, pet waste, and sprinkler heads before arrival.

Liability & Damage

By submitting a booking request and paying a deposit, the customer agrees that Primary Source Consulting is released from liability for any injury or damages resulting from use of the bounce house. The customer is responsible for any damage to the unit beyond normal wear and tear, including damage caused by silly string, food, drinks, or well water.

Delivery & Pickup

Standard delivery is Saturday at 9am. Standard pickup is Sunday at 6pm. Customer must be present at both delivery and pickup. If no one is available at delivery, a $50 redelivery fee applies.
